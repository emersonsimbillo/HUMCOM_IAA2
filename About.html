WELLSRPING HIGH SCHOOL

The Start of wellspring high school

Wellspring High School Inc. - Capas Tarlac, Philippines Wellspring High School is a private co-ed secondary school in Capas, Tarlac. It has been in continuous operation since 2002 and has then been a home for hundreds of students coming from all over the different municipalities in Tarlac and a source of strong-willed, determined and motivated high school graduates.

The term "wellspring" means a source of continuous supply. By this definition, Wellspring High School branched out from the roots of Montessori School of St. Nicholas to serve the mission of continuously providing the kind of quality education that elementary graduates experienced at MSSN.

Guided by the Latin phrase "suaviter in modo, fortiter in re" which literally means gently in manner, strongly in deed, Wellspring High School Inc. provides its students the kind of learning where they interact with others and give positive impact in a gentle way. It is an educational institution that perpetuates the precepts of knowledge, love, and enlightenment. It hopes to make students feel free in expressing themselves and their respective interests so that they may attain personal growth and self-actualization, keeping in mind their limits and reponsibilities. Truly, this is the home of students who were molded to become successful and responsible citizens and are proud to call themselves WHSians!
